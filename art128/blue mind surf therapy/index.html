Blue Mind Surf Therapy

Our research-based surf therapy programs is rooted in somatic, trauma-informed, eco, and community therapy models.

Our Program

Pulling from collective trainings and experience in trauma informed practices, somatic therapy, expressive arts therapy, positive psychology theory of change, and eco therapy. Along with receiving surf coaching, both in person as well as two individualized video coaching sessions, participants will engage in embodied learning through mindfulness, mental health awareness and tools, developing advanced surf knowledge, empowerment, and community.

Surf Therapy Sessions

Our research based two week Surf Therapy program is built to support you in developing your own sustainable ocean practice to enhance your mental health. During the weekly sessions, participants are invited to explore and integrate their therapy and surf goals held within the support of trained facilitators, volunteers and fellow participants. Equipment is provided and all ocean experiences are welcome.

Meet The Facilitators

The program is hosted by a Surf Therapy Facilitator, a licensed therapist, and a Surf Safety Each facilitator's goal is to maintain the utmost highest level of physical and psychological safety for all participants.
